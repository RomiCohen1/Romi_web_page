Dance


Dance serves as a universal language of expression, where movements reveal the innermost emotions and connect across cultures.
Ballet, with its structured elegance and storied traditions, offers a foundation of discipline and beauty in the dance world.
Contemporary dance breaks these classical boundaries, incorporating a blend of styles to explore new realms of creativity and physical expression.

I started dancing when I was five years old, and for years excelling in it was a goal I set for myself.
