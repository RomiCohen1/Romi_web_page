Dance


Dancing for me is first of all a way to express myself,to be happy, to laugh, to be angry, to cry, to breathe, and above all to live and feel alive.


I started dancing for the first time through the world of ballet, which became my first love and accompanied me to the army. Ballet was more than a hobby for me and excelling in it was a goal I set for myself.


In retrospect, I understand that thanks to the dance world I acquired skills that help me today - perseverance, determination, resilience, and time management


Over the years, I have danced classical ballet, and modern dance, salsa and Bachata.
